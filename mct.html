ROBO War

×

Rules:

  • A team may consist of a maximum of 4 participants all from the same institute (or) may be from different institute.
  • Maximum duration of each round will be 5 minutes. Any team not ready at the time specified will be disqualified.
  • Weight of the bot should not be exceed 50 kg.
  • Fight are classified into three categories of weight.
  • A robot is declared victorious if its opponent is immobilized (or) out of the arena.
  • If both robots survive the 5 minutes at the point, the robot with the higher hit points wins. The winner moves on, the loser is eliminated.
  • If the bot is wired then the wires coming out of the bot should be stacked as a single unit.
  • All efforts must be made to protect battery terminals from a direct shot and causing a battery fire, failure to do so will cause direct disqualification.
  • Battery eliminators are allowed and 230V AC power supply will be available at the venue for the eliminators.
  • The mean voltage should not exceed 24 volt DC at any point of time.
  • Bots will be categorized according to the number of participants.
  • The organizers reserve the right to change any of the above rules as they deem fit.
  • Change in any rules, if any will be highlighted on the website and notified to the registered teams.
  • If you have a robot that thus not fit within the categorized set forth in these rules, please contact the event organizer.

Line follower

×

Rules

  • The task for line follower robots is to drive through the track marked with black line as fast as possible.
  • The Objective of this contest for a robot is to follow a black line on a white background, without losing the line, and navigating several 90 degree turns. The robot which complete the course in the shortest period of time while accurately tracking the course line from start to finish wins.
  • It must be an “Autonomous Robot”
  • Maximum of 2 members per team. Further details provided on spot.

    • ROUND 1:

     The track consists of only curvy turns.

    ROUND 2:

     The track consists of a combination of curvy and sharp turns(less than equal to right angle)

    ROUND 3: 

    The track is like puzzle for the robot should solve and get out from. (Round 3 will be conducted only if necessary)

Robo Soccer

×

Rules:

  • Each team will have to defend the goal on its side as well as score the goal on the opposite side.
  • Each team can have a maximum of 5 team members.
  • The weight limit of the robot for this competition is 5 Kg. .
  • Each robot should not Exceed by maximum 30cm.
  • Winner is announced by the points they scored.
  • Bot should not be designed with any actuation mechanism in term of offencing the opposite bot.
  • In case of jam up of robots for more than 30 seconds the robots will have to kick-off again at the order of the Refree.
  • In case of any discrepancy the final decision rests in the hands of co-ordinators. The organizers reserve the right to change any of the above rules as they deem fit. Change in any rules, if any will be highlighted on the website and notified to the registered teams.
