Optimum Architectus

×

LEVEL 1: Who is the best Mason?

  • Task 1: The participants have to guess the height of the shown structure by just looking.(do not use any measuring instruments).
  • Task 2: The participants have to guess the width of the shown structure by just looking.(do not use any measuring instruments).
  • Task 3: The participants have to guess the area of the shown material (e.g. Brick) by just looking.(do not use any measuring instruments).

LEVEL 2: Cross the Twist

10 reasonable questions related to civil engineering will be asked.
(e.g)Which building is safe Trussed or Roofed?

LEVEL 3: Crack the puzzle

Civil related terms (technical terms, famous buildings, monuments etc.,) will be displayed in jumbled format. At instant time clues will be given (3 clues). LEVEL 4: Just remember-rules
A series of 10 pictures will be shown, participants should look at the picture for the
given time(30 sec’s).Questions will be raised with respect to the shown pictures.

LEVEL 5 : Master of plan-rules.

A plan has to be drawn in a A4 sheet for the given specification.(the specifications are given indirectly).

Event rules :

  • Team can have maximum of 2 members.
  • There is no restriction on the number of team from the same college
  • There is no elimination in the any of the levels

SITE ALL ROUNDER:

×

Description :

This event is related to onsite works and techniques which are used in current construction works. There will be three rounds prelims, flow through and rapid knots. There will be elimination in first two rounds and in last round first two places will be selected. First round will be conducted with civil engineering oriented technical terms, second round will be conducted with pipe joints as plumbing layouts and the last round will be conducted with reinforcement details by practically.

Event rules :

  • Number of students can participate in this event (2-3 members)
  • Tutorials will be provide, participants should know about the event thoroughly
  • There is no restriction on the number of team from the same college
  • Elimination process starts after each level.
